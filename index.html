Welcome to SMCC

Vision

Saint Michael College of Caraga envisions to be a university by 2035 and upholds spiritual formation and excellence in teaching, service, and research.

Missions

  1. SMCC shall provide spiritual formation and learning culture that will ensure the students with excellent and rewarding learning experience that transform lives, abound spirituality, develop skills and prepare future leaders;

  2. SMCC shall engage to dynamic, innovative, and interdisciplinary researches that are publishable to advance and achieve institutional initiatives;

  3. SMCC shall commit to serve the diverse and local communities in fostering innovations through service-learning that enhances reciprocal community partnerships for spiritual and social development.

About SMCC

Saint Michael College of Caraga (SMCC) is a leading institution committed to providing quality education and holistic development to its students.

Services

  1. Academic Advising: Guidance and support for students in planning their academic paths and achieving their educational goals.
  2. Counseling Services: Mental health counseling and support for students facing personal or academic challenges.
  3. Library and Research Support: Access to library resources, research assistance, and workshops on information literacy.
  4. Technology Support: Assistance with IT services, computer labs, and software applications for academic use.
  5. Financial Aid and Scholarship Assistance: Guidance on financial aid options, scholarship opportunities, and assistance with financial planning for education.
  6. Diversity and Inclusion Initiatives: Support for promoting diversity, equity, and inclusion on campus through programming, events, and resources.
